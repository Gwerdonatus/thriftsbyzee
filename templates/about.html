Your trusted destination for affordable, stylish thrift fits for ladies.

Who We Are

Welcome to ThriftbyZee, an online thrift store offering trendy and high-quality fits at incredibly affordable prices. We understand the need for looking good without breaking the bank, which is why we have dedicated ourselves to curating the best thrift items for women. Our store has grown over the years and now offers delivery to all 36 states of Nigeria, bringing chic styles right to your doorstep.

Zara Manga - Founder & CEO

Zara Manga is the visionary behind ThriftbyZee. She has successfully managed the business for over 6 years, building a loyal community of fashion-conscious women who trust in her curation of stylish thrift fits. With Zara's expertise and passion for fashion, ThriftbyZee has become one of the most beloved thrift stores in Jos, Nigeria, and beyond.
